Verified Deep Learning [CS 839 / S20]
Aws Albarghouthi
Hours: TBD in CS 6363


Deep learning has transformed the way we think of software and what it can do. But deep neural networks are fragile and their behaviors are often surprising. In many settings, we need to provide formal guarantees on the safety, security, correctness, or robustness of neural networks. This course covers foundational ideas from formal verification and their application to deep learning.
